Volleyball

Volleyball is a 6-person team game. Players have to hit the ball in the air over a net that is 7 ft 11 in high. A point is scored when the ball touches the ground on one side of the court. Each side can only hit the ball 3 times before hitting it over the net.

Tips and Tricks

How to Serve

When doing an overhead serve, you want to throw the ball to a good height. With your non-dominant hand, aim where the ball is, and with your dominant hand, bring your elbow back, swing with an open palm, and hit the ball.

How to Bump

Being able to bump pass is fundamental, as this is the start of any play. The better you can pass, the better the team can succeed.

How to Set

Setting is important to get the ball to a specific part of the court. The better you can set, the more you can support your team.
